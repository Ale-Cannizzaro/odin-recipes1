Burrito Recipe

Photo by Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Description

A fast and easy recipe to prepare delicious meat and veggies burritos.
Enjoy the best flavours of the tex mex cuisine!

Ingredients:

  • Tortillas: 4
  • Ground beef: 10,5oz
  • Onions: 1
  • Bell peppers: 1
  • Black beens: 9oz
  • Corn: 5oz
  • Tomato purée: 10,5oz
  • Chili peppers: 1
  • Extra virgin oil: as needed
  • Salt: to taste
  • Pepper: to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat a generous drizzle of olive oil in a non-stick pan. Finely chop an onion and sauté it in the oil. When the onion starts to turn translucent, add the ground beef, season with salt and pepper, and cook for about five minutes.

  2. Wash and slice the bell peppers into strips. Add them to the meat and stir. Cover with a lid and let them cook for a few minutes over low heat to allow the flavors to combine.

  3. Once the peppers are tender, add the tomato purée, black beans, and the chopped chili pepper (seeded). Cover and continue cooking. Finally, add the corn and mix well to combine all the ingredients. Cook until you get a thick sauce with a rich flavor.

  4. Heat the tortillas in a non-stick pan over medium heat, one at a time. Once soft, transfer to a plate and place a spoonful of the prepared filling in the center. Roll the tortilla up tightly into a wrap.

Enjoy!
